A Boutique Fan Experience

Welcome to the Ivalice Alliance community! We're a group of fans who enjoy discussing all things interesting, from video games and anime to music and bar culture. Inspired by the iconic Final Fantasy franchise, we use a character system where members can pick a name from the series as their persona, adding an extra layer of fun to our conversations. While Final Fantasy is a common theme, we also chat about a variety of other topics.

Our community operates as a small Discord server, offering a relaxed and engaging space where members can easily connect. A smaller setting allows for more meaningful conversations, ensures everyone has a voice, and fosters a strong sense of camaraderie.

Whether you're a long-time Final Fantasy fan or just looking for a place to chat with others who share your interests, you're welcome here. Pick a character, join the discussion, and enjoy the conversation!
