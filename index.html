A Boutique Fan Experience

Welcome to the Ivalice Alliance community! We are a group of fans who are passionate about discussing all things interesting and unique, from video games and anime to music and bar culture. Our community is inspired by the iconic Final Fantasy franchise, and as such, we have a character system where members can pick a name from the series as their persona. This adds an extra layer of fun and immersion to our conversations, which extend beyond just Final Fantasy. We enjoy talking about a wide range of other topics as well.

One of the things that sets our community apart is that we operate as a small Discord server. This intimate setting fosters a tight-knit and supportive atmosphere, allowing members to easily connect and engage with one another. The benefits of a small server include more meaningful interactions, closer relationships, and the chance to be heard in conversations without being drowned out by a large number of participants. It also helps to cultivate a genuine sense of camaraderie among members.

Whether you're a fan of the series or just looking for a place to chat about your interests with like-minded people, you'll find a welcoming and inclusive community in our small server. We embrace diversity and respect all members, regardless of their background or level of knowledge. So come on in, pick your character, and join the conversation! We can't wait to get to know you and discuss all of our shared interests within this cozy and friendly environment.
