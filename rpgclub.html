RPG Club is an engaging and social activity that can be best described as a book club for role-playing games (RPGs). At the start of the last week of each month, our group selects three or four potential games to play. By Wednesday of that week, we vote on which game to play for the following month.

During the month-long activity, all participants are highly encouraged to play and discuss the selected game while also sharing and contributing their favorite media related to the game. Our community is open to all types of contributions, including fan art, videos, pictures, and even romhacks.

We also offer exciting group activities such as watch parties for related media, like watching the Final Fantasy XV movie or the anime prequel. Another way to contribute is through game streaming, which can be an incredibly fun way to share your experiences and strategies with other members of the community.

Although none of these contributions are mandatory, we believe that they are excellent ways to make the most of the activity and enhance the experience. Join us and discover a welcoming community of RPG enthusiasts who love to read, play, and share their passion for this genre!
