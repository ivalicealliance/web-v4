RPG Club is a community event that operates like a club for role playing games (RPGs). Near the end of each month, our group nominates and votes on games for future play. The game selected for the current month automatically carries over to the next poll, allowing it to be played for multiple months.

Throughout the event, participants are encouraged to play and discuss the selected game while sharing their favorite related media. Contributions can include fan art, videos, images, romhacks, and game streaming as a way to share experiences and strategies with the community.

Although participation in these activities is optional, they provide great opportunities to enhance the overall experience. Join us to explore a community of RPG enthusiasts who enjoy playing and sharing their passion for this genre!
