We're thrilled that you're interested in joining! To complete the signup process, please review this agreement and follow these instructions.

Requirements

  • Discord account
  • Selection of a Final Fantasy Character

Character Selection

To fully participate, you must select a Final Fantasy Character as your persona. Any characters listed on the Final Fantasy wiki or confirmed to appear in any Final Fantasy title, including spin offs, are acceptable choices.

Joining the Discord Server

Follow these steps to join our Discord server:

  1. Use this invite link to access the server
  2. Inform the channel of the Final Fantasy Character you have selected
  3. Wait for confirmation from a Judge or Consul

Once these steps are completed, you will be granted full access to our Discord server.

Thank you for your interest, and we look forward to your participation!

Questions or Assistance

For any questions or assistance, please contact a Judge or Consul. They will be happy to help clarify the signup process or address any other concerns you may have.
