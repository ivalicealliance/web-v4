We're glad you're interested in joining! To complete the signup process, please review this agreement and follow the instructions.

Requirements

  • Discord account
  • Selection of a Final Fantasy Character

Character Selection

To fully participate, select a Final Fantasy Character as your persona. Any character listed on the Final Fantasy wiki or confirmed to appear in any Final Fantasy title, including spin-offs, is acceptable.

Joining the Discord Server

Follow these steps to join our Discord server:

  1. Use this invite link to access the server
  2. Notify the channel of the Final Fantasy Character you have selected
  3. Wait for confirmation from a Judge or Consul

Once these steps are completed, you will receive full access to our Discord server.

Thank you for your interest. We look forward to your participation!

Questions or Assistance

For any questions or assistance, please contact a Judge or Consul. They are available to help clarify the signup process or address any concerns.
