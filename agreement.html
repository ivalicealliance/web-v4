By joining, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in a warning, temporary suspension of privileges, or removal from the community. These terms may change over time. If you disagree with any changes, you are free to leave the community.

{{ title }}

Respect Others

Treat all community members with respect. Abusive language, bullying, and unfounded accusations will not be tolerated.

Use a Final Fantasy Character

Your display name should represent a character from the Final Fantasy franchise. This requirement applies to all community content. While it is preferred that you use this character name on other platforms as well, it is not mandatory.

No Spam

Avoid spamming. For more details on what is considered spam, see this definition.
