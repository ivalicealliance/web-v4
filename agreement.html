By joining, you agree to the following rules, which if violated, could result in a warning, suspension of community privileges, or removal from the community. These terms may be updated at any time. If you do not agree with any changes, you have the option to leave the community.

{{ title }}

Respect All

Maintain a respectful demeanor towards other members of the community. Derogatory language, bullying, and false accusations are not tolerated.

Use Final Fantasy Character

Your display name must represent a character from the Final Fantasy franchise. This is required on any content associated with the community. While it is preferred that you use this character name on other platforms, it is not mandatory.

No Spam

Do not engage in spamming activities, as defined by wikipedia.
